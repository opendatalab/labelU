# Basic config

1. Click on the "New Task" button on the "Task List" home page to jump to the "Basic Configuration" page of "New Task".

![](/en/no-task.png)

2. Fill in the "Task Name", check the "Data Type" (you can choose from image, video, or audio), optionally fill in the "Task Description" and "Task Tips". Then click the "Next" button in the upper right corner to save the basic configuration information and enter the "Data Import" page. 

![](/en/task-basic-config.jpeg)

:::tip
The "Task Tips" filled in will be displayed in the upper right corner of the "Start Annotation" page to provide tips for the annotator. Annotators can click the "Task Tips" button to view the information filled in during basic configuration.
:::
